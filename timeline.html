Steph's Accomplishments

  • June 25th 2009 Steph was selected as the 7th overall pick in the NBA Draft.
  • June 2015 Steph Curry win's his first NBA Championship and MVP.
  • June 2016 Steph Curry becomes first person to win unanimous MVP.
  • 2017-2018 Steph Curry wins back to back NBA Championships.
  • 2021 Steph Curry averages more than 30 ppg the season and secures his second NBA scoring title.
